Homemade Chilli

Description:

Homemade chili is delicious and goes great with cornbread or over corn chips for a chili pie. I like to use pinto beans.

Ingredients:

  • beef
  • onion
  • canned goods
  • spices

Steps:

  1. cook the beef and the onion
  2. add the remaining ingredients, then bring to a boil
  3. reduce the heat and let simmer for 15 minutes
