Buffalo Chicken Dip

Description:

This creamy Buffalo chicken dip taste just like Buffalo wings Perfect for parties and game day, it's best serve hot with crackers and celery sticks.

Ingredients:

  • chicken
  • cream cheese
  • ranch dressing
  • hot sauce
  • cheese

Steps:

  1. heat the chicken and hot pepper sauce
  2. mix cheddar cheese
  3. cover and cook on low until dip is hot and bubbly
  4. serve with celery sticks and crackers
